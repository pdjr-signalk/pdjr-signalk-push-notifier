push-notifier plugin - email & web-push registration

This page allows you to subscribe to email and web-push notification services so that you will receive a message and/or web-push notification when configured alarm notifications appear in Signal K.

Note that both of these services require configuration in Signal K and may require Internet connectivity to operate in a meaningful way. See here for more information.

Email subscription/unsubscription

Enter your email address into both text input fields and click the appropriate button.

 

Web-push subscription/unsubscription

Click the appropriate button.

Note that you will be required to authorise web-push subscription in your web-browser and a subscription must be made on every device on which you wish to enable this service.

Web-push registration is disabled because it was not possible to obtain the required VAPID public key from the server.
